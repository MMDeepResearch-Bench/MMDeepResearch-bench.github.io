Evaluation pipeline

A unified, interpretable 3-stage pipeline combining report quality, citation support, and image-grounded integrity: FLAE, TRACE, and a gated MOSAIC stage.

FLAE

Formula‚ÄìLLM hybrid: reproducible text features + task-aware judging, fused with an adaptive coefficient.

TRACE

Verifies claim‚ÄìURL support and enforces multimodal prompt faithfulness via visual evidence fidelity.

MOSAIC (Gated)

Checks integrity between visual-referenced statements and visual artifacts; activated only when earlier stages pass thresholds.

Key results & findings

Main experiments evaluate a diverse set of models and deep research agents; results highlight cross-metric trade-offs between writing quality, citation discipline, and multimodal grounding.

Highlighted observations

  • Clear trade-offs between generation quality and multimodal grounding.
  • Top-ranked system is driven by strong evidence quality while keeping competitive multimodal alignment.
  • Even strong models can diverge: better visual grounding does not guarantee better citation discipline.
